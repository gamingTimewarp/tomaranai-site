The Five Treasures of Inpei Kaguya

November 2022
This story is a mystery from six different perspectives. It is intended to be experiened as an immersive experience, optionally being managed by a seventh person. You will need to print out each of the below files and distribute them randomly or by some other method to all participants. The participants will then get together to discuss what happened, when it happened, and figure out all the details of the mystery by process of elimination. You may also act out the events of each character live, but be careful that it is not clear when the culprit kills the victim. At the end, the victim is not allowed to comment on the events occuring, but can confirm them once the other parties have taken a vote. The culprit is allowed to lie to attempt to decieve the other members into voting for the wrong assumptions. To win, the votes must be correct for the culprit, the location, and the time of death.
