Privacy Policy — Grimoire Atziluth

Effective: 24 March 2026

Grimoire Atziluth is developed independently by gamingTimewarp. This policy describes what data the app handles and how.

Short version: Grimoire Atziluth is a fully offline app. It stores your data locally on your device and never transmits anything to any server. There are no analytics, no telemetry, and no outbound network connections made by the app.

Data stored on your device

The following is saved to a local SQLite database on your device:

  • Divination readings — cards drawn, spread used, notes, timestamps, and the astrological sky snapshot at the time of the reading
  • Daily readings
  • Journal entries
  • Natal chart profiles — name, birth date, time, and location as you enter them
  • Custom entities, traditions, and decks you create
  • Spaced-repetition (SRS) learning progress
  • App preferences — theme, accessibility settings, tradition choices — stored in the device's local browser storage

None of this data leaves your device unless you explicitly use the built-in export feature to create a backup file. The backup file is saved wherever you choose on your own device or storage.

What is not collected

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics or behavioural tracking
  • No crash reports or error logs sent to any external service
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data beyond what you voluntarily enter into a natal chart profile
  • No contact details of any kind

Third-party services

The app makes no outbound network connections at runtime. All astronomical calculations are performed locally using a bundled offline ephemeris (astronomy-engine by Don Cross, MIT licence). All reference data — tarot, qabalah, astrology, runes, geomancy, I Ching, Lenormand — is included with the app at install time. No internet connection is required for any feature.

Your control over your data

  • Export: Settings → Data → Export to save a portable backup of your readings and journal.
  • Delete individual items: Readings and journal entries can be deleted within the app.
  • Delete everything: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

Children

Grimoire Atziluth is not directed at children under 13. The app does not collect data from any user, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL and the effective date above will be changed. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
